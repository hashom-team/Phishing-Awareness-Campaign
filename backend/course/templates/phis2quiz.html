Phishing and Spoofing Quiz

Test your knowledge about phishing and spoofing!

1. What is the primary method used in phishing attacks to deceive victims?

  • Exploiting software vulnerabilities.
  • Social engineering tactics.
  • Malware distribution.
  • Network intrusion.

2. Which of the following is a sophisticated technique used in email spoofing?

  • Using a legitimate email service provider.
  • Implementing SPF and DKIM records.
  • Creating a subdomain that mimics a trusted domain.
  • Sending emails from a secure server.

3. What is the purpose of a "phishing kit"?

  • To educate users about phishing.
  • To provide tools for creating phishing websites.
  • To report phishing attempts to authorities.
  • To enhance email security.

4. Which of the following is a sign that a website may be spoofed?

  • The website has a valid SSL certificate.
  • The URL contains unusual characters or misspellings.
  • The website loads quickly.
  • The site has a professional design.

5. What is the best practice to avoid falling victim to phishing attacks?

  • Always click on links in emails from known contacts.
  • Use two-factor authentication on sensitive accounts.
  • Share your passwords with trusted friends.
  • Ignore all security warnings.
