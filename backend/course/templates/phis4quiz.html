10 Ways to Prevent Phishing Scams Quiz

Test your knowledge about preventing phishing scams!

1. What is the most effective way to stay informed about new phishing techniques?

  • Ignoring emails from unknown senders.
  • Subscribing to cybersecurity newsletters and updates.
  • Relying on friends to inform you.
  • Only checking news once a year.

2. When should you be cautious about clicking links in emails?

  • When the email is from a known contact.
  • When the email contains urgent language.
  • When the email is from a company you trust.
  • You should never be cautious.

3. What should you do if you receive a pop-up asking for personal information?

  • Click the "cancel" button.
  • Close the browser tab immediately.
  • Enter the information to see what happens.
  • Take a screenshot and share it on social media.

4. How can you verify a website's security before entering sensitive information?

  • Look for "http" in the URL.
  • Check for "https" and a padlock icon in the address bar.
  • Trust any website that looks professional.
  • Only enter information on websites you have used before.

5. What is the purpose of using an anti-phishing toolbar?

  • To block all pop-ups.
  • To alert you when you visit a known phishing site.
  • To enhance your browsing speed.
  • To automatically fill in forms.
