Phishing and Identity Theft Quiz

Test your knowledge about phishing and identity theft!

1. What is spear phishing?

  • A targeted phishing attack aimed at a specific individual or organization.
  • A general phishing attack sent to a large number of people.
  • A phishing attack that uses social media.
  • A method of securing personal information.

2. Which of the following is a common tactic used in identity theft?

  • Using stolen credit card information.
  • Creating fake social media profiles.
  • Phishing for personal information.
  • All of the above.

3. What is a common sign that your identity may have been compromised?

  • Receiving bills for services you did not use.
  • Getting a new credit card in the mail that you did not apply for.
  • Being denied credit for no apparent reason.
  • All of the above.

4. Which of the following is a method used by phishers to obtain personal information?

  • Sending fake emails that appear to be from legitimate companies.
  • Creating counterfeit websites that mimic real ones.
  • Using social engineering techniques to manipulate victims.
  • All of the above.

5. What is the best way to protect yourself from identity theft?

  • Use the same password for all your accounts.
  • Regularly monitor your financial statements and credit reports.
  • Share your passwords with trusted friends.
  • Ignore all security warnings.
